Soya Chunks

Description

Soya Chunks aka Meal Maker Curry is a delicious Indian dish made with soya nuggets, spices, onions, tomatoes and herbs. This vegan mock meat curry is the ultimate comfort food! Super delicious, easy to make and is sure to please everyone. If you love something tasty and meaty without having to eat the real meat, this is absolutely for you. Serve it over rice, pulao, jeera rice or with roti, paratha, naan. It simply tastes the best with anything.

Ingredients

  • 100 gm soya chunks (meal maker/soya nuggets)
  • 250 ml hot water
  • 1 onion and 5 green chilis
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons Cooking Oil
  • ¼ teaspoon Garam masala powder
  • 1½ teaspoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder

Steps

  1. Boil the water(250 ml) with some salt and after add soya chunks and leave for 10 min
  2. Remove soya chunks from water after it gets cold by pressing with hands
  3. Now turn ON the stove and put a pan/curry bowl, add Oil and wait for 1 min (in medium flame)
  4. Add onion and green chili pieces, and fry them little
  5. Add garam masala powder (in low flame)
  6. Add soya chunks and fry little bit(in medium flame)
  7. After add ginger garlic paste, salt, turmeric and mix them well and leave for 5 min (in low flame)
  8. Add some water to bring tasty gravy and leave for 5 min with lid closed
  9. Add red chili powder and coriander powder and mix them well
  10. Low the flame and leave it for 10 min
  11. Now turn OFF the stove, thats it! "tasty" and "healthy" meal maker curry is ready!!
